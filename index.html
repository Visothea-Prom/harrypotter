History of Making Harry Potter

This video will tell the process of Making Harry Potter by J.K. Rowling and the life of J.K. Rowling

  • 01 How many Harry Potter copies are sold world wide?

    Over 500 million copies of the Harry Potter series had been sold worldwide. However, this number may have increased since then, given the enduring popularity of the books. J.K. Rowling's series has become one of the best-selling book series in history, captivating readers of all ages across the globe.

  • 02 Average Ratings of Harry Potter books series?

    The Harry Potter film series has received high ratings on Rotten Tomatoes, with most of the films having scores ranging from 70% to 96%. Overall, the series has been well-received by both critics and audiences alike.

  • 03 Who is the main villain in the Harry Potter series?

    The main villain in the Harry Potter series is Lord Voldemort, also known as Tom Riddle. He is a dark wizard who seeks to conquer the wizarding world, achieve immortality, and establish a regime of pure-blood supremacy. Voldemort is responsible for numerous acts of violence, including the murder of Harry Potter's parents and the deaths of many other characters throughout the series. His quest for power and domination drives much of the conflict in the story as Harry Potter and his friends oppose him in their efforts to protect the wizarding world.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Harry Potter or Fantastic Beasts better?

    This is a matter of opinion, but fans love both for different reasons. Harry Potter follows the journey of a boy wizard, while Fantastic Beasts dives into the magical world and its creatures.

  • What is the difference between a witch and a wizard?

    In the Harry Potter universe, there is no meaningful difference between witches and wizards. Both can perform magic.

  • In what order should I read or watch Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts?

    Most people recommend reading or watching Harry Potter first, as it came out before Fantastic Beasts and lays the groundwork for the wizarding world.

  • Who is Albus Dumbledore?

    Albus Dumbledore is a wise and powerful wizard who appears in both Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts. He plays a mentor role to Harry Potter and is considered one of the greatest wizards of all time.

  • What are some of the most popular magical creatures in the Wizarding World?

    There are hundreds of fascinating creatures, but some fan favorites include house-elves like Dobby, goblins like Griphook, and of course, fantastic beasts like Fawkes the phoenix and Hedwig the owl.

  • Will there be any more Harry Potter or Fantastic Beasts movies?

    There are currently no plans for new movies in either series, but the Wizarding World is always expanding with new stories and games!
